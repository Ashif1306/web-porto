How long does a website take to build?

The timeline for website development varies based on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 2-3 months or more.

Website costs vary widely based on requirements, features, and complexity. Basic websites start from $1,000, while custom solutions with advanced functionality can range from $5,000 to $20,000+.
